This is a security app which aims to provide some assistance to user at the time of crisis by sending your location to your near-ones.
The app opens to a screen displaying few icons .The function of each icon is really easy to understand and is briefly described below.

SETTINGS
This icon helps you to store contacts of those people whom you want to contact in crisis.

INSTANT MESSAGE
This provides a service of sending your location to the contacts you saved in settings.You just have to click the button and your location will be messaged to all the saved contacts.

GPS TRACKER
This starts a service and keeps on sending your location to the contacts saved in settings after an interval of 10 minutes.

ALARM
This starts a service that keeps on ringing an alarm continuously that may prove useful in repelling any attacker.

Hope u find this app useful.
